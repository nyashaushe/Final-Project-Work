About Travel Explorer

Many travelers struggle with finding relevant and up-to-date travel information for their destinations. The Travel Explorer web application aims to provide a seamless experience by aggregating essential travel details, including weather forecasts, local attractions, and currency exchange rates.

This project will help travelers plan their trips more efficiently by offering real-time information from reliable sources. The goal is to create a user-friendly, visually appealing web app that simplifies travel planning.
